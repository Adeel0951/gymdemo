FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free trials?

Yes! We offer a 3-day free trial for all new members. Book your trial through our contact form or call us directly.

Is parking available?

Absolutely. We provide free parking for all members with over 100 dedicated spots in our secure lot.

Are trainers certified?

All our trainers hold nationally recognized certifications including NASM, ACE, and CrossFit Level 1+ credentials.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical or travel reasons at no extra cost.

Do you have female trainers?

Yes, we have several certified female trainers specializing in yoga, nutrition, HIIT, and personal training.
